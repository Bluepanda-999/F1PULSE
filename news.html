FIA: Liam Lawson not at fault in incident with marshals

FIA: Lawson cleared after Mexico incident

Summary: During the race in Mexico City, Liam Lawson narrowly avoided track marshals who crossed the circuit. The FIA opened an investigation, while the Automobile Club of Mexico (OMDAI Sport Mexico) initially accused the Racing Bulls driver of causing a dangerous situation.

  • The FIA issued an evening statement responding to the Mexican club.
  • Initial findings indicate Liam Lawson was not at fault.
  • Telemetry shows car No. 30 slowed appropriately under double yellow flags.
  • A full, transparent review with OMDAI and Racing Bulls is ongoing.
